# Public Safety Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity work focused on 911 / Public Safety Answering Point (PSAP) 
environments, combining hands-on training delivery with independent 
academic research. This focus is informed by my experience working in 
a county 911 dispatch center, which gave me firsthand exposure to the 
operational realities that this work examines.

## Contents

- **[`training-and-outreach/`](./training-and-outreach)** — A cybersecurity 
  awareness training program designed and delivered to a regional PSAP, 
  developed as a capstone project. Includes the sanitized project paper 
  and findings brief.

- **[`research/`](./research)** — Independent and coursework-based research 
  applying established security frameworks to PSAP cyber resilience, 
  including an original interview-based investigation into telephony 
  denial of service (TDoS) preparedness.

## About the author

Michael Keuhlen is a cybersecurity graduate (AAS, BAS — Columbia Basin 
College) with CompTIA A+, Security+, and Qualys VMDR Specialist 
certifications, and prior experience working in public safety 
communications.
